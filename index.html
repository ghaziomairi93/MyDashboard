📖 User Guide

🌦 Weather Widget Top Left

  • Tap to open city search
  • Type your city and select from results
  • Location saves locally and persists across visits
  • Shows real-time temperature in Celsius

⏱ Live Clock Top Right

  • Real-time digital clock with seconds and milliseconds
  • Timezone synced with your selected city
  • Shows date and timezone abbreviation below

📊 Stats Row Row 2

  • Unread Mail: Auto-updates after Outlook sign-in
  • Events Today: Auto-updates after Google sign-in

🔋 Battery Row 3 Left

  • Shows your device battery level in real-time
  • Green = above 50%, Orange = 20–50%, Red = below 20%
  • Shows ⚡ Charging when plugged in
  • Note: iOS Safari may show N/A due to system restrictions — check Control Center

✅ Uncompleted Tasks Row 3 Right

  • Live count of pending tasks for the selected day
  • Updates instantly when you check off a task

📅 Google Calendar

  • Tap Sign In to connect your Google account
  • Events load automatically for the selected day
  • Use the 7-day date bar to browse upcoming days
  • Tap ↻ Refresh to reload manually

🔔 Reminders

  • Tap + to add a task for the selected day
  • Check the box to mark a task complete
  • Tap Clear Done to remove all completed tasks
  • Tasks are stored locally on your device
  • Browse future dates using the 7-day bar

✉️ Outlook Inbox

  • Tap Sign In — you'll be redirected to Microsoft login
  • Returns here automatically after sign-in
  • Shows your 15 most recent unread emails only
  • Compatible with Hotmail, Outlook.com, and Office 365
  • Tap ↻ Refresh to reload inbox

📅 7-Day Navigation

  • Both Calendar and Reminders share a 7-day date bar
  • Tap any date pill to switch the view for that day
  • Today's date is highlighted by default

🗑 Clear My Data

  • Single click → confirmation prompt appears
  • Wipes tasks, location, Outlook and Google sessions
  • Page reloads automatically after clearing

💬 Support

  • Tap Support button to open the contact form
  • Fill in name, email, and message
  • Your message is sent securely and anonymously
  • Response time: 24–48 hours

💬 Contact Support

Have a question or found a bug? Send a message and we'll get back to you within 24–48 hours. Your email is kept private.
