📖 User Guide

🌦 Weather Top Left

  • Tap to search and set your city
  • Shows real-time temperature in Celsius
  • Location saved locally and persists across visits

⏱ Live Clock Top Right

  • Real-time clock with seconds and milliseconds
  • Synced to your selected city's timezone

📊 Stats Row

  • Tap Unread Mail to jump to inbox
  • Tap Events Today to jump to calendar
  • Numbers update automatically after sign-in

⏳ Countdown

  • Tap ＋ Add Event to set a name, date and optional time
  • Live countdown shows Years / Days / Hours / Minutes
  • Tap ✎ Change Event to edit the existing countdown
  • Saved locally — persists across visits, cleared by Clear My Data

💡 Wisdom Quote

  • Shows a motivational quote with author and year
  • Tap anywhere or the ↻ button for a new random quote
  • Saved locally — persists across visits

✅ Tasks Count

  • Shows uncompleted tasks for today
  • Tap to jump to Reminders section

🔔 Reminders

  • Use the date and time picker to select when the task is due
  • Tap + to add a task — title is saved with the selected time
  • Checkbox marks task done — stays visible with strikethrough
  • Tap 🗑 to permanently delete a task
  • Tap Clear Done to remove all completed tasks
  • Tasks stored locally and persist across visits

📅 Google Calendar

  • Tap Sign In to connect your Google account
  • Use the date picker to jump to any date
  • Use the 7-day bar to browse upcoming days
  • Tap + Add Event to create an event — saved to your real Google Calendar instantly when signed in
  • Events created without sign-in are saved locally with a LOCAL badge
  • Tap any event to open the full details in the Google Calendar app
  • Tap 🗑 next to an event to delete it — also removes it from Google Calendar server
  • Tap ↻ Refresh to reload events from Google

✉️ Outlook Inbox

  • Tap Sign In — redirected to Microsoft login
  • Returns here automatically after sign-in
  • Shows 15 most recent unread emails
  • Tap any email to open it directly in your mail app
  • Unread count stat reflects your real inbox total
  • Session persists across page refreshes

🗑 Clear My Data

  • Single tap → confirmation prompt
  • Clears tasks, location, countdown, and all sessions
  • Page reloads automatically

💬 Support

  • Fill name, email, and message
  • Sent securely — your support address stays hidden
  • Response within 24–48 hours

💬 Contact Support

Have a question or found a bug? We'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.
